# Supply Chain Domain

The Supply Chain domain adds inventory and supplier reliability analytics to the
local OpenLakeForge POC. It is intentionally related to the Sales seed data by
sharing product identifiers.

The domain follows the same domain-oriented product asset layout as Sales:
`domain.yaml` owns governance metadata, while product assets live side by side
under `contracts/`, `examples/`, `extract/`, `transformations/`, `pipelines/`,
and `reports/`.
